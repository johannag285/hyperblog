Barranquilla (American Spanish: [baraŋˈkiʝa] (About this soundlisten)) is the capital district of Atlántico
Department in Colombia. It is located near the Caribbean Sea and is the largest city and port in the northern 
Caribbean Coast region; as of 2018 it had a population of 1,232,766,[3] making it Colombia's fourth-most populous 
city after Bogotá, Medellín and Cali.
